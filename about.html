About me

I am currently pursuing my PhD degree in Computer Science at Vanderbilt University, under the superviosion of Prof. Bobby Bodenheimer. I am currently investigating how asymmetric locomotion methods can affect the acquired spatial knowledge of collaborative navigators in a shared virtual environment. Apart from my research, I love to do solo travel and capture serene photos of places, moments, and people. I also love to draw and paint. You can find the collection of photos captured by me on my instagram profile. You are welcome to check my social networking pages to know me better.
