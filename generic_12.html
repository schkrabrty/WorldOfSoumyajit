VR Showcase 2022 at Vanderbilt University

And the winner group is "Accessibility Quest"...

At the end of the Fall 2022 semester, students from the Projects in Virtual Reality Design (CS-4249/5249) course participated in the departmental final project showcase alongside peers from other project-based courses such as Software Engineering and Cyber-Physical Systems. I was delighted that the project "Accessibility Quest" won first prize. The game explored a key accessibility challenge—how individuals using wheelchairs navigate virtual environments. As the Graduate Teaching Assistant for the course, I was proud to have guided the team in developing several critical gameplay mechanics.
