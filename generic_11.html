Teleoperation Using Video Prediction by Taking Network Delay & Jitter into Account

My summer 2025 internship project at Nokia Bell Labs

Teleoperation becomes challenging under real-world network delays, which can fluctuate unpredictably. To address this, I developed a Unity- and WebRTC-based prototype that measures real-time delay and predicts future video frames, enabling smoother remote control. This work was guided by Dr. Vasu Vasudevan, Elangovan Manickam, Mike Coss, Dr. Larry O'Gorman, and Dr. Lars Andersson. I received the "Global Student Program 2025 Outstanding Innovation Award" for this work from Dr. Thierry Klein, President of Bell Labs Solutions Research.
